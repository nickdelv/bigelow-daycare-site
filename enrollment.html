Enrollment

Tuition, schedules, and how to apply.

Bigelow is currently accepting applications for enrollment. The best first step is to schedule a tour — we'll walk you through everything from there.

How to Enroll

Three steps to get started.

01
Schedule a tour

The best way to get a feel for Bigelow is to visit in person. Tours are at 9am, last 30–45 minutes, and children are welcome.

Request a tour →

02
Submit an application

After your tour, you're ready to apply. Submit your application online or print it out. There is a $50 application fee, waived for families with state child care vouchers.

Submit an application →

03
Sign your contract

Enrollment is confirmed with a signed 12-month contract and a one-month tuition deposit. The deposit is applied toward your August tuition the following year.

Financial Assistance

Quality childcare, available to all families.

Bigelow works with the Somerville Partnership for Young Children (SPYC) to connect families with financial assistance resources — including the City of Somerville's Child Care Access and Affordability Program — to ensure every child in Somerville has access to quality early care.

Bigelow is open Monday through Friday, 8:30am–5:30pm. The core program day runs 8:30am–3:30pm. Extended day care until 5:30pm is available as an add-on.

Bigelow is open 48 weeks a year. We close for standard holidays, late August through early September, the week between Christmas and New Year's, and April vacation week. We also close for three professional development days per year.

As a cooperative, families are active members of the school community. Each family participates in a monthly classroom work shift, attends community and classroom meetings (roughly every other month), and joins one parent committee — hiring, finance, web and technology, physical space, and others. One clean-up day each fall and spring is also expected.

Bigelow is committed to making participation work for a wide range of family situations. If your schedule or circumstances make any of these commitments difficult, reach out — there are always other ways to be involved.

Bigelow understands that schedules don't always allow for on-site participation. Families who aren't able to attend classroom shifts are encouraged to reach out to Director Dawn Lorino — she's glad to identify other ways to contribute that fit your life.
